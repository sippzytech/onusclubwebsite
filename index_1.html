How it works

Three taps from first visit to a regular.

No hardware to buy, no app for your customers to install. If your staff can scan a QR code, you can run OnUsClub.

01

They add the card

A customer scans your code and adds their loyalty card straight to Apple or Google Wallet. One tap — nothing to download.

02

You scan at the counter

They show the card from their wallet. Your staff scans it and adds a stamp from a simple dashboard. Seconds, every time.

03

The reward's on you

Stamps add up, the card updates itself, and a wallet notification nudges them back. The free one is on the house.
